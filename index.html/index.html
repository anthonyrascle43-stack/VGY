VGY / ON REPETITION

a new way to abuse playlists.

A strange thing has been happening in music. We have endless access, yet we keep playing the same thirty songs on repeat, like a nervous tick. Algorithms observe this behaviour and quietly reinforce it, folding each half-remembered track back into a perfectly optimised loop.

VGY exists to do the opposite. It treats your history like a physical archive: a stack of mislabelled records, bootleg CDs, unnamed playlists and late-night experiments. Instead of smoothing the noise, it leans into it — reorganising your listening into issues, chapters, strange little side-stories.
